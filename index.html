About Me

I'm Mike Russell, born in 1992. I am a seasoned digital advertising expert, specializing in Facebook Ads, TikTok Ads, and Google Ads. With over 5 years of experience, I have had the privilege of helping businesses of all sizes achieve their marketing goals (and beyond), by providing significant growth via online PPC ads.

My journey in online PPC and advertising began as a solo freelancer. Through dedication, continuous learning, and a passion for ROI and A/B testing, I have transformed my solo venture into a PPC agency. Today, my team and I deliver outstanding results and growth for clients, across Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and Facebook Ads.

To date, we have run ads for over 100 companies, and managed a total of over 10 million dollars of advertising budget. I am also a contributor for AdStage, where I share free advice and strategies regarding the world of advertising.
